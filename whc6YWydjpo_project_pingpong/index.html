Rules For The Game:

  1. This game only works on web.
  2. For starting the game press  Enter.
  3. For moving the rod right press  D.
  4. For moving the rod left press  A.
  5. The rod which have ball you are in that rod.
  6. The target of the game is moving the ball in between the two rods.The rod which misses to hold/catch the ball will be considered as lost.
  7. Your score and result will be publishised by alert when game is over.
